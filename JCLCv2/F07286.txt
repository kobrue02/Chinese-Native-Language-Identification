
　　
　　幸福
　　建立在诚实正直，卓有成效的工作，贡献和自尊的基础上的幸福才会长久。幸福不终点，而是一个过程，这个过程需要连续不断的诚实正直、卓有成效地工作。它让你真正有益于他人，让你意识到自身的价值。
　　Long-term happiness is based on honesty ,productive work, contribution,and seft-esteem.Happness is not an end ,it is a process.It is a continous process of honest,productive work which makes a real contribution to other and makes you feel you are useful ,worthy person.
